🏥 Major NHS Trust 📡 800 Access Points ⏱️ 60-Day Project 👥 2-Person Team 🌙 Night Shift Access

The Challenge

A major NHS Foundation Trust needed comprehensive wireless network assessment to optimize patient care connectivity

Critical Connectivity Issues
  • Patient Care Disruption: WiFi dead zones affecting medical devices and patient monitoring
  • Clinical Workflow Impact: Staff unable to access patient records in certain areas
  • Emergency Response: Communication blackspots in critical care areas
  • Infection Control: Limited access to isolation wards and infectious disease units
Complex Healthcare Environment
  • Operational Constraints: Hospital operating 24/7 with minimal disruption allowed
  • Security Requirements: High-security areas requiring special access protocols
  • Patient Privacy: Sensitive areas requiring careful survey methodology
  • Infrastructure Age: Legacy network with mixed equipment generations
