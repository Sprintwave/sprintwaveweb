Exam Overview

The Implementing and Operating Cisco Wireless Core Technologies v1.0 (WLCOR 350-101) is a 120-minute exam associated with the CCNP and CCIE Wireless Certification.

This exam tests a candidate's knowledge of implementing core wireless technologies including:

  • Radio frequency fundamentals
  • 802.11 technology fundamentals
  • Client connectivity
  • Wireless monitoring and management
  • Automation and AI

Certification Path

CCNP Wireless: Pass this exam (350-101) plus one concentration exam

CCIE Wireless: This exam serves as the written qualification exam

Detailed content outline for each exam domain

1.0 RF Fundamentals

1.1 Describe the propagation of radio waves
  • Free space path loss
  • Reflection, refraction, diffraction
  • Multipath and Fresnel zones
  • Indoor vs outdoor propagation
1.2 Describe RF signal measurements
  • 1.2.a Signal strength - RSSI, transmit power, receive sensitivity
  • 1.2.b Interference, noise, rogue AP - Sources and mitigation
  • 1.2.c Device capabilities - Smartphones, laptops, tablets
  • 1.2.d SNR - Signal-to-Noise Ratio calculations and impact
1.3 Describe the principles of RF mathematics
  • dB, dBm, dBi calculations
  • Link budget analysis
  • Power calculations
1.4 Describe Wi-Fi antenna characteristics
  • Antenna patterns and gain
  • Polarization and diversity
  • Beamforming and MIMO
