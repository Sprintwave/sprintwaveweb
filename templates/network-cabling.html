Cable Types & Applications

Cat5e Cabling

Cost-Effective
  • Speed: Up to 1 Gbps
  • Frequency: 100 MHz
  • Distance: 100 meters
  • Applications: Basic networking, VoIP
Best for: Small offices, basic internet access, cost-sensitive installations

Cat6 Cabling

Most Popular
  • Speed: Up to 10 Gbps (55m)
  • Frequency: 250 MHz
  • Distance: 100 meters (1 Gbps)
  • Applications: High-speed networking, PoE+
Best for: Modern offices, high-bandwidth applications, future-proofing

Premium
  • Speed: Up to 10 Gbps

  • Applications: 10G networking, PoE++
